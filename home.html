    Donate

    Blood and organ donation are acts of selfless giving that have the power to save and enhance countless lives. By donating blood, you can help patients in need of transfusions. By donating organs, you can give someone a second chance at life. Your donation can have a profound impact on the lives of others. A single blood donation can help up to three people. A single organ donor can save the lives of up to eight people. Donating blood or organs is a safe and easy process. If you are considering donating, please talk to your doctor. Make the decision to donate today. You could be the one to save a life.

    Donate Organs

    kidney

    Kidney transplantation is a surgical procedure in which a diseased kidney is replaced with a healthy kidney from a deceased or living donor. Kidney transplantation is a life-saving procedure for people with end-stage kidney disease.

    Heart

    Heart transplantation is a surgical procedure in which a diseased or failing heart is replaced with a healthy heart from a deceased donor. Heart transplantation is a life-saving procedure for people with end-stage heart failure.

    Lungs

    Lung transplantation is a surgical procedure in which a diseased or failing lung is replaced with a healthy lung from a deceased donor. Lung transplantation is a life-saving procedure for people with end-stage lung disease.

    Eye

    Eye donation is the gift of sight. Corneas from donated eyes can be used to restore vision in people with corneal blindness. Corneal transplantation is a safe and effective procedure that can restore vision to people of all ages.

    Pancreas

    Pancreas transplantation is a surgical procedure in which a diseased or failing pancreas is replaced with a healthy pancreas from a deceased donor. Pancreas transplantation is a life-saving procedure for people with type 1 diabetes.
